What Is DECA?

DECA is an international organization that prepares high school and college students for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management. It focuses on leadership development, business skills, and entrepreneurship through competitions, conferences, and hands-on experiences.



Key Features of DECA

  1. Competitions: Students participate in role-play events, case studies, and presentations to solve real-world business challenges. There are multiple levels of competition, in which a student must place and advance through each level to attend the next. While some states differ, this is the typical order of competition.
    • District competition
    • State competition
    • Regional competition
    • International Career Development Conference

    Watch the video about the International Competition in 2025!




  2. Leadership Development: Opportunities to build teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

  3. Professional Development: Focuses on career readiness and networking with industry professionals.

  4. Membership: Over 250,000 members across the world in high schools and colleges.

  5. Varying Interests: DECA offers many different event that students can study and compete in. There are categories of each event. Here are the individual events.
    • Business Management & Administration
      1. Business Services Marketing (BSM)
      2. Entrepreneurship (ENT)
      3. Resources Management (HRM)
    • Finance
      1. Accounting Applications (ACT)
      2. Finance (FIN)
      3. Financial Consulting (FCE)
    • Hospitality and Tourism
      1. Hotel and Lodging Management (HLM)
      2. Restaurant and Food Service Management (RFSM)
      3. Travel and Tourism Management (TTM)
    • Marketing
      1. Apparel and Accessories Marketing (AAM)
      2. Automotive Services Marketing (ASM)
      3. Food Marketing (FMS)
      4. Retail Merchandising (RMS)
      5. Sports and Entertainment Marketing (SEM)
      6. Marketing Communications Series (MCS)
